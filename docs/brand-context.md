Unapologetically choosing yourself by taking the time and the courage to rise through the murky waters, and finally let the world bask in awe at the very best version of you. And in that acceptance, everything changes.

When we trust the process.
When we get comfortable with being uncomfortable.
When we do the work.
This is where we grow.
This is where we rise.
This is where we glow.

This is KAMALA.

## The Itinerary

### Friday
- **7:00pm — Arrival & check in.** Refreshments on arrival. Welcome pack and itinerary on the bed. Unpack at your own pace.
- **8:00pm — Welcome dinner.** Family-sharing style, seasonal. First connection over food.
- **9:15pm — Cacao ceremony, breathwork & journaling.** Candles, blankets, low light. Ceremonial-grade cacao. Gentle breathwork. Private journaling on two prompts (*What do I want to leave behind this weekend? What do I want to take home?*). Closes with a quiet collective intention.
- **10:00pm — Lights down.** Chamomile and valerian teas available.

### Saturday — the heart of the retreat
- **From 8:30am — Wake naturally, drinks station open.** Matcha, lions mane coffee, pressed juices, turmeric lattes, herbal teas, antioxidant shots, coconut water.
- **9:00am — Breakfast.** Generous, relaxed, family-style.
- **10:00am — Welcome & weekend walkthrough (15 min).** MC sets the tone, answers questions.
- **10:30am — Opening gong meditation (90 min).** Gentle movement, short breathwork, then lying down. Gongs, chimes, sacred sound, sage. No words.
- **12:30pm — Gentle transition & grazing table.**
- **1:30pm — Energy release workshop (2.5 hrs).** Somatic breathwork and guided movement. Deep emotional/physical release, held safely.
- **4:00pm — Free time (3 hrs).** Walk the grounds, fire pit, chess, nap, read, journal. Cinema room open with curated documentaries. Drinks station open.
- **7:00pm — Group dinner.** Candlelit, family sharing, organic. Menu curated to the day's activities.
- **8:30pm — Reishi tea ceremony, breathwork & journaling.** Reishi tea (mushroom of calm). Short breathwork. Journaling prompts (*What shifted today? What surprised me? What feels different in my body from yesterday?*).
- **10:00pm — Lights down.** Silence from this point.

### Sunday — slow, spacious, unhurried
- **8:00am — Morning yoga (optional but encouraged).** 60 min restorative.
- **9:00am — Breakfast.** Last group breakfast.
- **10:00am — Pool floating sound healing.** 12m warm indoor pool, sound healer performs while you float. Towels, robes, flotation aids provided. Spa complex exclusively the group's.
- **11:30am — Free time, spa, massage & rest.** Pool, jacuzzi, sauna, steam room until 2:30pm. Optional add-on massage delivered to your room.
- **1:00pm — Farewell grazing lunch.**
- **2:00pm — Farewell & take-home gifts.** Each guest leaves with their journal, a KAMALA Reishi set, and a pressed juice for the journey.
- **3:00pm — Latest checkout.**
