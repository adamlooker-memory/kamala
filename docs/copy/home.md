## What is Kamala

Kamala is a small, considered retreat held in private estates in the UK.

Three days. A handful of guests. Real food, real silence, and the kind of work that asks something of you.

We don't promise transformation. We hold the space for it.

## Signature experiences

<!-- Four-up grid. Each tile gets a placeholder image and the line below. -->

**Cacao ceremony.** Ceremonial-grade cacao, candlelight, and a journal. The first softening of the weekend.

**Gong meditation.** Ninety minutes of sacred sound. Lie down. Don't speak. Let it move through you.

**Somatic release.** Breath and movement, held safely. The work the body has been waiting to do.

**Pool floating sound healing.** A warm indoor pool, a sound healer, and your weight given over to the water.
