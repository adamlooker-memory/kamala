## How we choose

Every practitioner on a Kamala weekend is chosen with care.

We're not assembling a programme of workshops. We're holding a single arc across three days, and each person we bring in has to fit the shape of that arc — what comes before them, what comes after, what the room will need by the time they arrive.

We work with practitioners we've sat with ourselves. People with depth in their craft, who know how to hold a room when the room gets heavy, and who don't perform their lineage. Skill, and humility, in equal measure.

The line-up evolves retreat to retreat. Some practitioners return; new ones join when the weekend calls for them.
